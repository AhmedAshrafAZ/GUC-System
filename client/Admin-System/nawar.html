About nawar

Nawwar aims at introducing a new way of learning to Egyptian students through animated videos and other services. Nawwar project aspires to break the old educational cycle and replace it by a more effective way to learn that takes advantage of the technological revolution that our current decade is characterized by. We explain main concepts and details associated with various lessons in multiple fields using fun animation. To ensure that the content provided is of high quality, we depend on more than one source of information such as books, lectures materials and T/A notes.
