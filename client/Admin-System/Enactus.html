About Enactus

Previously known as SIFE-GUC . The name changed to ENACTUS to reaffirm our long-standing commitment to using entrepreneurial action as a catalyst for progress. Enactus represents the premier business and higher education network with more than 70,500 university students, 1700 active universities in 36 countries and support provided by hundreds of leading companies. Enactus brings together a diverse network of university students, academic professionals and industry leaders who share the vision of creating better more sustainable world though the positive power of business. By contributing their talents to projects that improve the lives of people worldwide, Enactus participants are demonstrating the individuals with knowledge and passion for business can be powerful force for change. ENtrepreneurial - having the perspective to see an opportunityand talent to create value from that opportunity . ACTion - the willingness to do something and the commitment to see it through even when the outcome is not guaranteed . US - a collection of people who see themselves connected in some important way; individuals that are part of a greater whole.
