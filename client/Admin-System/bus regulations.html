Bus regulations:
1- Registration in the transportation service is per academic year not per semester. (Students cannot register for using GUC buses during one semester only).
2- The student is only entitled to pay for the transportation after paying the first installment of the tuition fees.
3-Financial Aid and Scholarship are not taken into consideration regarding the payment of transportation.
4- Transportation fees are not transferable or refundable.
5- Transportation fees are not transferable or refundable.
6- Please be informed that changes in the transportation fees may occur according to changes in the gas market.
7- once you register payment must be done you cannot pay later else you will have to re-register
