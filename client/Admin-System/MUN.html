About MUN

GUCMUN was created to enhance the personal, intellectual and social skills of the German University in Cairo and Egyptian student, through modeling the United Nations in an annual conference. We believe that providing a high quality learning experience to students will enable them to realize their potentials and develop their competences to becoming better individuals capable of helping one another and their societies. Since 2009, GUCMUN is a journey that changed the way every person in it perceives our society and the world so grab this opportunity and become an agent of change because as we always say: "Determined to fulfill a dream, dream of being the change" (GUCMUN Charter)
