About ASB

AYBGUC is a student-run activities group from the German University in Cairo representing AYB, a sustainable development NGO. Alashanek ya Balady- GUC (AYB-GUC) is a franchise under AYB sustained development association. As we believe that the family is the seed of development for any community, hence we follow the “family-based” development approach by which we target poor communities with our different development programs.

Developing Matareya district through various educational, cultural, social, charitable, environmental, political and economical projects
